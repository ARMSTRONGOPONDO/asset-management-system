Smart Asset Manager

Know where every asset is, who has it, and what it’s worth – in one clear view.

Built for universities and organizations that are tired of chasing asset information across spreadsheets, notebooks, and memory. This dashboard keeps everything in one organized place for day‑to‑day work and audits.

  • Register laptops, lab equipment, and furniture in seconds.
  • See who is responsible for each item and where it lives.
  • Log maintenance and repairs so nothing is forgotten.
  • Mark items as disposed while keeping a clean history trail.
  • Promote trusted staff to admin directly from the dashboard.

Asset records often start as many different spreadsheets, notebooks, or verbal promises. This system brings those pieces together into one clear history for each item, so audits, handovers, and management decisions become simpler and faster.
