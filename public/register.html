Create an account for your asset workspace

Give your team a shared, up-to-date view of every important item you manage.

Use this registration page to create your personal login. When you are the first person to register in a new database, you will automatically become an Admin. After that, existing admins can promote or demote users from inside the dashboard.

  • Register with your real name and work email so colleagues can recognize your updates.
  • Each login is personal, so actions like creating, editing, or disposing assets can be traced to a user.
  • Admins can manage who has admin rights without touching the database or calling IT.

Tip: use a strong password you can remember. This account gives you access to sensitive information about institution-owned assets.
